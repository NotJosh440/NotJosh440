About Us

We Are Pittsford Robotics

A FIRST Team #3181 based in Pittsford, New York.

We give students the opportunity to explore STEM, Art & Media, and Business with hands-on experiences and competitions.




STEM



Our team strives to promote STEM education in our community. Students gain hands on experiences in mechanical, electical, and software engineering by building a life-size and fully functioning robot that completes a series of tasks for each season's competition. Each year, we host outreach events such as robot demonstrations at local places and contribute to a recycling drive for metal and electronics.

BUSINESS



Being a non-profit organization, our student-ran business team oversees funding, sponsor relations, resource allocations, and community outreach. Each year, the team manages thousands of dollars needed to build the robot and enter competitions around the nation. Students who are interested in finance, marketing, and management explore the intersection between busniess and STEM through our team.

Art & Media



Pittsford Robotics give aspiring artists an oppertunity to apply their creativity and skills in real-word applications. Art and media tackle projects like designing team apparel, adding content to social media pages, and making a safety video of the safety award each year.
